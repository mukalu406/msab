Uniting Muslim Students in Busoga Region

The Muslim Students Association of Busoga (MSAB) is a regional organization dedicated to serving the needs of Muslim students across all educational institutions in the Busoga region.

Established in 2023, our association has grown to become a vital platform for Muslim students to connect, learn, and contribute to society while maintaining strong Islamic values.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a platform for Muslim students in Busoga region to grow spiritually, intellectually, and socially while upholding Islamic values and contributing positively to society.

Our Vision

To become the leading Muslim students' association in Eastern Uganda, recognized for nurturing future leaders who excel both in religious knowledge and secular education.
